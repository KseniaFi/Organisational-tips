Pre-reading Task: Activating Knowledge & Prediction

1) Do you ever have trouble finding things at home or at work? Give an example.
2) What are some methods you or your family use to keep things organised? Are they effective?
3) Why do you think it can be difficult to stay organized?
4) What advice would you give someone who wants to keep their space tidier?
5) Based on the article’s title, what kinds of “life-changing tips” do you think will be suggested?
